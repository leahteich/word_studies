https://www.ims.uni-stuttgart.de/en/research/resources/experiment-data/antonym-synonym-dataset/

This dataset contains 600 adjective pairs (300 antonymous pairs and 300 synonymous pairs), 
700 noun pairs (350 antonymous pairs and 350 synonymous pairs) and 800 verb pairs 
(400 antonymous pairs and 400 synonymous pairs).
